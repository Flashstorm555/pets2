Battle royale game

4+ players, shorter play times are best

Basic rules

  • Everyone gets a backpack with stuff in, last one standing wins.
  • Once you die you go back home.
  • If you’re in the storm text the group chat, you’re dead.

Setup

  1. You join one chat where all the stuff you need to know in the game is posted.

  2. During the game, a picture of the area with circles are sent. If you don’t get inside the circle in time, you die.

  3. There is a second chat where everyone’s locations are on, you can look at this after you’ve died

Equipment

All the info on equipment is in the packs
