Step 1: Combat

We are going to need several ships in this guide, but for this step just about any will do! To start off, we are going to need a combat ship to gather the nessesary material we need to really supercharge our ship, Focus crystals.

The best place to grab these insert alliteration here are ship wreckages from resource extraction sites or RES for short. Any level will do, but make sure you're not outgunned or it's a one way trip to the rebuy screen

Also, now would be a good time to outfit your ship for exploration, or at least shed some extra weight as we will be doing a lot of FSD jumping from here on out.

Step 2: Data

Next on our shopping list tour of the bubble, we have to get 9 Datamined wake exeptions. These shouldn't be to hard to come across as all you have to do is scan a ship's wake after they jump out of a system, but can take some time if you don't know what you are doing. The best place to do this would be to go to a High population system with a wake scanner.

Step 3: Surface prospecting

For this step we'll need to use a tool on eddb.io to find some arsenic. Simply click this link, fill in the details below and click Find Bodies.

Then to make things easier, click the collumn with the percentage of arsenic. This should list all planets with a high amount of what we're looking for so it should be quicker to find. Let's pick a system and Jump!

I found about X Arsenic in XX minuets

Once you are on the planet, you need to drive around in your srv and shoot the rocks you find with your turret. If you're lucky a bit of arsenic should come out. Then just target it and scoop it up.

Step 4:Preperation

Now comes the tedious bit, unlocking the engineer,Felicity farseer, we are using to upgrade our fsd. I found that the quickest way to gain rep with her is to hand in exploration data. This shouldn't be too hard if we use Road2Riches, and should get us a bit extra cash to buy a ship too.
